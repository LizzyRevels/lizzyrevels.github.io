Wind Speed vs. Latitude

The Latitude vs. Wind Speed plot shows that most cities in both Northern and Southern Hemispheres have wind speeds that usually stay below 10 to 15 mph. There are a few outliers in the Southern Hemisphere with higher wind speeds of 25 and 40 mph. In the Northern Hemisphere at latitudes 20 to 80 degrees there is a small percentage of latitudes where wind speeds are in the 20 to 35 mph range.
