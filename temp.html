Temperature vs. Latitude

The Latitude vs Temperature plot shows that the latitudes with higher temperatures, for example in the 80 to 100 degree range are closer to the equator, and latitudes with lower temperatures are further north into the Northern Hemisphere. The northern latitudes starting at about 20 degrees have temperatures that sharply decline as the latitudes more north.
